===== Short Description =====
Run and jump. Help Yan to save his girlfriend and friends from Evil Dragon.


===== Full Description =====

Yan's world is amazing platformer (side-scroller) game. Jump and run!

Amazing adventures with many levels, enemies and big bosses to challenge skills of the player. Each level brings new experience.

Yan lived peaceful life with his beautiful girlfriend and friends. Suddenly Evil Dragon appears. He destroyed Yan's village and kidnapped Yan's girlfriend...
After Yan woke up he went to release his girlfriend and friends. Adventure has begun.

Please help Yan to defeat Evil Dragon and release his girlfriend.
